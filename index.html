The Vision of Future

Bangladesh's Bangabandhu-1 satellite, launched in 2018, enhances telecommunications, broadcasting, and emergency services across the country. It ensures rural connectivity and disaster management support. Plans for Bangabandhu-2 aim to expand capabilities in remote sensing and space research, boosting national development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The future of technology in Bangladesh promises rapid growth with advancements in AI, 5G, and smart infrastructure. Widespread digitalization will enhance education, healthcare, and e-governance, bridging the urban-rural divide. A booming startup ecosystem, fueled by innovation, will drive sustainable development. Renewable energy tech and smart cities will shape a greener future. With a focus on tech-driven skills, Bangladesh is poised to become a South Asian innovation hub, empowering its youth and economy.
